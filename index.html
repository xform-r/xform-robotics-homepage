Why It Matters

Loneliness isn't just silence — it's a health crisis.

Exi brings warmth, connection, and safety back into everyday life.

More than one in three seniors worldwide experience chronic loneliness.

Studies show that social isolation increases the risk of depression by 47% and early mortality by 26%.

Many live alone for hours or days without interaction or support.

Exi bridges that gap. It listens, talks, and observes with gentle awareness — restoring daily routine, comfort, and connection through natural conversation and presence.
